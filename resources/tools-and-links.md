# Startup Tools and Resources

Curated list of tools, frameworks, and resources for building startups.

## Legal & Incorporation

### Incorporation & Legal

- **Carta** — Equity management, cap tables, option management
- **Stripe Atlas** — US C-corp incorporation (simple and cheap)
- **Clerky** — Delaware incorporation and documents
- **Cooley** — Legal templates and guides for startups
- **NOLO** — Self-help legal resources
- **Entity Plus** — Registered agent and filing services

### Contracts & Templates

- **Cooley StartupDocs** — Free startup legal templates (nondisclosures, employment agreements, equity docs)
- **Y Combinator Startup Law** — Open-sourced legal documents
- **Docracy** — Community-shared contracts and agreements

## Quick Reference: Essential Tools by Stage

### Pre-Launch (Validation)
- Customer interview notes (Google Sheets or Notion)
- Simple pitch deck (Google Slides)
- Landing page (Webflow or no-code)

### MVP & Launch
- Source control (GitHub)
- Project management (Linear)
- Customer research (Typeform, Intercom)
- Analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)

### Early Growth
- CRM (Pipedrive, HubSpot)
- Email (Mailchimp or SendGrid)
- Business metrics (Metabase, custom dashboards)
- Hiring (Lever)

### Growth Stage
- Full analytics suite (Amplitude, Looker)
- Advanced CRM (Salesforce)
- CPM and ROI (SEMrush, similar)
- Equity management (Carta)

### Late Stage
- Sophisticated accounting (NetSuite, Ariba)
- Advanced HR/People (BambooHR, Workday)
- Enterprise analytics (Tableau, Looker)
- Strategic tools for your industry
