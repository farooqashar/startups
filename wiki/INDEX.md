## Navigation Tips

1. **Start with context**: If you're new, read [Startup Fundamentals](startup-fundamentals.md) first
2. **Follow your stage**: Jump to the section for your current stage
3. **Use case studies**: Real examples are in [Case Studies](../history/case-studies.md)
4. **Reference quickly**: Use [Quick Reference](../resources/quick-reference.md) for numbers and templates
5. **Avoid mistakes**: Consult [Common Mistakes](../notes/common-mistakes.md) before big decisions
6. **Learn patterns**: Study [Startup Patterns](../notes/startup-patterns.md) for what repeats across companies

This knowledge base is a living document. As new learnings emerge or better resources are found, it should be updated. When something contradicts what's written here, the real-world data should win and the knowledge base should be updated to reflect it.
